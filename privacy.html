Introduction

KRITTIKA Apps ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Ulfat mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

Information We Collect

Information We DO NOT Collect:

Ulfat is designed with your privacy in mind. We DO NOT collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device information
  • Usage analytics
  • Contacts or photos
  • Any other personal data

Information Stored Locally:

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Favorites: Shayari/Ghazals you mark as favorites are saved in your device's local storage
  • Preferences: App settings and preferences (disclaimer acceptance, etc.)
  • No Server Upload: This data never leaves your device

How We Use Information

Since we don't collect any personal information, there is nothing to use, share, or sell.

The locally stored data (favorites and preferences) is used solely to:

  • Remember your favorite shayari
  • Improve your app experience
  • Avoid showing the disclaimer popup repeatedly

Third-Party Services

Google AdSense (If Enabled):

If advertisements are enabled in the future, Google AdSense may collect certain information as per their privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Currently, no advertisements are active in the App.

No Analytics:

We do not use any analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.). Your usage of the App is completely private.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • We do not have access to your data
  • Your data is as secure as your device
  • Uninstalling the app will delete all locally stored data

Children's Privacy

Our App does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The App is safe for all ages.

Content Disclaimer

The shayari, ghazals, and poems in this App are collected from various public sources on the internet. We respect the original creators and their work.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in the App and on our website: https://pandeabhijitv-ux.github.io/ulfat/

Consent

By using Ulfat, you consent to this Privacy Policy.
