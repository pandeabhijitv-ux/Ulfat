## Information We Collect

### Information We DO NOT Collect:
Ulfat is designed with your privacy in mind. We **DO NOT** collect, store, or transmit any of the following:
- Personal information (name, email, phone number)
- Location data
- Device information
- Usage analytics
- Contacts or photos
- Any other personal data

### Information Stored Locally:
The App stores the following data **locally on your device only**:
- **Favorites:** Shayari/Ghazals you mark as favorites are saved in your device's local storage
- **Preferences:** App settings and preferences (disclaimer acceptance, etc.)
- **No Server Upload:** This data never leaves your device

## How We Use Information

Since we don't collect any personal information, there is nothing to use, share, or sell.

The locally stored data (favorites and preferences) is used solely to:
- Remember your favorite shayari
- Improve your app experience
- Avoid showing the disclaimer popup repeatedly
